Incoming Transmission

A fest built on three verbs, not one theme.

CrescoScientiam began as a single-day science meet at DPS Megacity and has grown, edition after edition, into a two-day fest where science, storytelling, and strategy collide. Every event traces back to one of three instincts: the instinct to create something from nothing, the discipline to establish it under scrutiny, and the nerve to compete for it on the day.

  • 01A city-wide science fest hosted by Delhi Public School Megacity, Kolkata.
  • 02Fifteen events across Mythology, Cosmos and Eco — from chess to genetic engineering.
  • 03Five editions strong, growing from four events in 2019 to fifteen in 2026.
