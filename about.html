Cresco Scientiam is DPS Megacity's annual science fest for senior students across the city, designed to sharpen logical thinking without losing sight of imagination. Hosted every year by Delhi Public School Megacity, Kolkata, it runs on a simple belief: science taught only from a textbook is science half-learned.

Across its five editions, the fest has widened from a single afternoon of four events to a full two-day arena of twelve — spanning chemistry, mathematics, forensics, genetics, architecture, debate, drama, music and more. What hasn't changed is the format: real problems, real deadlines, real judges.

This year's theme, asks delegates to think past the immediate brief — to treat every event not as a task to finish, but as a frontier to push. Three theme-worlds — Mythology, Cosmos, Environment — hold the fifteen events together.

Our School

Delhi Public School Megacity

Delhi Public School Megacity is built around a straightforward idea: academic excellence and genuine curiosity aren't in tension, they reinforce each other. The school's science, robotics, and debate circuits regularly send delegates to city and national-level competitions — and Cresco Scientiam is the platform where that culture opens its doors to every other school in the city, once a year, for two days.
